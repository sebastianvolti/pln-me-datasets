   An apple is a fruit. Most apples are juicy and sweet. How can you eat an apple? You can bite into a fresh apple. Crunch! You can have applesauce as a healthy snack. You can eat an apple muffin or a piece of apple cake. You can have apple pancakes for breakfast. You can drink apple juice. It is sweet. You can try some apple pie. It smells good as it bakes. Did you know there were so many ways to eat apples?
