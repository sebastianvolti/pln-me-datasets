 1779MarbleHeight with socle: 20 1 /2 inches (52.1 cm)JEAN-ANTOINE HOUDONFrench, 1741–1828 This is a sculpture of Benjamin Franklin. It shows his face and the upper part of his body. It was made by a French artist. The artist’s name was Jean-Antoine Houdon. He made this sculpture in 1779. At that time, Benjamin Franklin was 72 years old. This sculpture was made from a hard, white stone. The stone is called marble. Houdon carved the marble to make it look like Benjamin Franklin. He made this sculpture without even asking Franklin to pose for him!
