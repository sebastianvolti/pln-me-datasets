   Money can be coins. Money can also be paper. People use money to buy things. That is called spending. People don’t spend all their money at the same time. They keep some for another time. That is called saving. Many people keep their money at a bank. A bank is a place that keeps money safe. Here are some names for money in the United States: A penny equals one cent.A nickel equals five cents.A dime equals 10 cents.A quarter equals 25 cents.A half-dollar equals 50 cents.One dollar equals 100 cents.
