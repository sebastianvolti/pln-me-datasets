Smithsonian Institute ArchivesPhoto of Theodore Roosevelt Theodore Roosevelt was an American president. He loved animals and nature. He did a lot to help protect land and create national forests while he was president. Roosevelt’s love of animals and nature started when he was young. Roosevelt was sick when he was a child. To get stronger, he spent a lot of time outdoors. He played a lot with animals. His love of animals lasted. When Roosevelt was an adult, he had kids. His kids also loved animals. His family lived in the White House when he was president. Roosevelt’s kids had many pets. The pets included dogs, raccoons, snakes, rats, guinea pigs, and a pony!
