Brigadeiros (bree-ga-DAY-ros) are little balls of chocolate fudge. They are often covered in sprinkles. People in Brazil love this dessert. Even adults in Brazil like to eat brigadeiros! They are given as gifts. They are also a very popular dessert at celebrations. People love to eat them at birthdays and weddings. Many Brazilians like to make brigadeiros at home. They are easy to make. There are also many shops that sell brigadeiros. These shops are not just in Brazil, though. People buy and eat brigadeiros around the world! Do you want to try a brigadeiro?
