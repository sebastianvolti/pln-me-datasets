     Can you tell a frog from a toad? Frogs and toads are alike in many ways. They both start their lives in water. Later, both can live on land or in water. Grown frogs and toads use lungs to breathe. Frogs and toads are also different. Frogs have narrow bodies. Toads have wide bodies. Most frogs have smooth, wet skin. Most toads have dry, bumpy skin. Frogs jump using their long back legs. Toads walk and hop using their short back legs.
