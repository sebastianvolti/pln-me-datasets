   The bald eagle is a symbol of the United States. A symbol is something that stands for something else. The bald eagle stands for the country’s strength. Here are some things we know about bald eagles. Bald eagles have wonderful sight. This helps them find food. They have strong claws to eat a fish or snake. Bald eagles are fast! Their powerful, wide wings help them fly very quickly. Bald eagles build huge nests from sticks and grass. They build nests high in trees. Baby eaglets hatch from eggs. Both parents bring the babies food. Soon the eaglets can fly and leave the nest.
