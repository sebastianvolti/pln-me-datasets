U.S. Department of State: GPA Photo Archive (CC BY-NC 2.0)This is a photo of Toni Morrison making a speech. Toni Morrison was an African American author. She wrote about Black Americans and their lives. Many of her characters experienced racism. She wrote about these characters dealing with those struggles. She also wrote about people understanding where they belong in their community. She used myth and poetry in her books. Many people around the world love her writing. She even won many awards for her writing. Toni Morrison wrote her whole life. Toni Morrison even wrote a few children’s books! She wrote them with her son. One of these books is called Who’s Got Game? : The Ant or the Grasshopper? Maybe you can find one of her children’s books in the library!
