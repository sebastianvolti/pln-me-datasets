Photo Credit: Tony Hisgett  Sam and his father went to an airport. "Those helicopters are cool!" Sam said. "Helicopters can land in small places," said his father. "They can also fly side to side." A man came over. "I can take you for a ride in one,” he said. Sam and his father buckled up. The blades on top of the helicopter spun around. Whirr! Whirr! The blades made a big sound. The helicopter shook. Then up they went. Sam looked down at his town. It was a great ride!
