 Benjamin Banneker Benjamin Banneker was an African American scientist. He lived many years ago. As a child, Benjamin loved to learn. He was very, very smart. His grandma taught him how to read. He taught himself math and science! As an adult, Benjamin worked on many things. He invented a clock made out of wood. It worked for over 40 years! He also helped plan what the shape of Washington, D.C., would be. Back then, Washington, D.C., was a new city. It would become the capital of the United States.  Benjamin also studied the sun, moon, and earth. He could tell how the sun, moon, and earth would move. He could also tell when they would move a certain way. He was right nearly all the time!
