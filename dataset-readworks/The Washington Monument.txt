   The Washington Monument is a special building. It is in Washington, DC. That is the capital of the United States. The monument was built to honor the United States’ first president, George Washington. It is a memorial to him. The monument is tall and skinny. It is made of marble and granite. Inside is a statue of George Washington. There is a staircase of 800 steps. Today, you can reach the top by riding in an elevator. At the top, you can look out windows to see in all directions. United States flags fly outside the Washington Monument. They are symbols of the United States.
