Groton School (CC BY 2.0)A squash player is getting ready to hit the small rubber ball. Have you ever heard of squash? It’s not just something you eat. There’s another squash. It’s a fun sport! Squash is a very popular sport in England, Egypt, and the United States. In squash, there are two or four players. They are in a court with four walls. They each have a small racquet. They take turns hitting a small rubber ball onto the four walls of the court. England Squash Masters (CC BY 2.0)These are squash racquets. The ball can only hit the floor once in each shot. But the ball can hit as many walls as the player wants. The walls of many squash courts are see-through. People can sit outside these squash courts to watch. Would you play squash?
