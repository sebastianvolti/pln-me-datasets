 Photo Credit: Kevin Collins Mr. Lee took his dog for a walk. His dog was dark brown. His name was Hunter. Mr. Lee and Hunter walked along a path. The path was by a field. Mr. Lee took off Hunter’s leash. Hunter ran and jumped. Suddenly Hunter zoomed into the tall grass. Mr. Lee could not see him. “Hunter!”  he called. Mr. Lee walked into the tall grass. He heard a strange sound. He stopped and noticed a terrible smell. He looked down as a skunk ran off. The skunk had sprayed Hunter! “Oh, Hunter!”  called Mr. Lee. “It is bath time for you!”
