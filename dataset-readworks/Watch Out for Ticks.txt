   Ticks are tiny bugs that live in woods and fields. They can be hard to see. Ticks can bite people. Some tick bites can make people sick. How can you stay safe from ticks? Have a grown-up spray your skin with child-safe bug spray.Wear long sleeves and pants in woods or fields.Tuck your pant bottoms into your socks.Check all over your body for ticks. Have a grown-up you trust help you. If you find a tick on you, let a grown-up take it off. Wash the spot well with soap and water.
