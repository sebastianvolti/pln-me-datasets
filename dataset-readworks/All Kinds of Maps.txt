   Maps are pictures with information about places. People use many kinds of maps. Some maps show a country and its parts. For example, a United States map would show all fifty states. It would also show capital cities. Other maps may show streets and roads in cities or towns. People use these maps to find their way. Many maps show where water and land can be found. The color blue is often used for rivers and lakes. Brown or green is used to show land. Weather maps show the weather in a place. Different colors or symbols stand for rain, snow, or sunny weather.
