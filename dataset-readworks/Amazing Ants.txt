   Ants can be found all over Earth. These insects have some amazing skills. Ants are very strong. An ant can lift a leaf that is much bigger than it is. Ants have big jaws. They use their jaws to squeeze juice out of their food. Then the ants throw the rest of the food away. Most ants live together in colonies. The queen ant lays eggs. The worker ants do other jobs. Some ants find food, such as leaves. Other ants protect the queen and her eggs.
