   Who made the first United States flag? No one knows for sure. There is a legend about a woman named Betsy Ross. The legend says that she made the first flag. A legend is a popular story that people tell others. It is usually about a person, but the story may not be true. Here’s the legend of Betsy Ross. George Washington visited Betsy Ross in 1776. Washington gave her a drawing of a flag. It had 13 red and white stripes. It also had 13 stars with six points. Washington wanted Ross to make a flag like the drawing. Ross did not like the stars with six points. She made a flag like the drawing. But she made stars with five points. Then the legend says her flag was the first United States flag.
