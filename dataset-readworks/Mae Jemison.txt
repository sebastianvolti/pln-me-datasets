Â Mae Jemison Have you ever thought about going to space? Perhaps you'd like to be an astronaut when you grow up. Mae Jemison knows what it's like being in space. She is the first African American woman astronaut. When Mae was in school, she loved to study science. She had wanted to visit space since she was a child. Mae made her dreams come true. In 1992, Mae flew into space with six other astronauts. She became the first African American woman in space!  But Mae is not only an astronaut. She is also a doctor and a dancer.
