   Tornadoes and hurricanes are big storms. They bring strong winds that can hurt people. The winds can damage trees, cars, and buildings. Storm chasers are people who watch these storms closely. They drive to where a storm is taking place. They take pictures. They use computers to follow the storm. They gather weather information. Storm chasers are carefully trained to do their work. They help us learn more about these kinds of storms. Their work also helps more people stay safe.
