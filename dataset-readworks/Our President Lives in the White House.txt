   Washington, D.C., is the capital of the United States of America. The White House is in Washington, D.C. The president and the president’s family live in the White House. The president’s pets live there too. The White House has 132 rooms. It has 35 bathrooms. It has a swimming pool and a movie theater. The White House has a special office. It is called the Oval Office. The president works in the Oval Office. The White House has a lot of color. It has a room named the Blue Room where the president meets many of his guests. It also has a Green Room and a Red Room! Both rooms are sitting rooms usually used to welcome guests or to celebrate an event.
