   Scientists solve problems. Do you have a problem? You can try to solve it. Just think like a scientist. First, think about the problem you want to solve. Ask questions about the problem. Take a look at it closely. Read books to learn more. Search for answers. Guess how you can solve it. Test out your idea. Did you solve the problem? If you did, that is great. If you did not, that is fine too. Keep trying out new ideas. One may solve the problem. Scientists test out many ideas. They do not give up easily.
