   William Kamkwamba grew up in Malawi. Malawi is a country in Africa. When William was a boy, his village had a problem. There was no rain. Food could not grow. Most people in the village were farmers. Many people were hungry. William went to the library. He read science books. He learned that windmills are machines. They can change wind power into electrical power. William learned that windmills could pump water. That could help his village grow food. William’s windmill idea helped solve his village’s problem!
