 Saturn is a planet. Saturn and seven other planets move around the sun. The solar system is the name for the sun, planets, and other smaller bodies that move around the sun. Saturn is the sixth planet from the sun. It is the second-biggest planet in the solar system. Jupiter is the largest. Saturn is one of a few planets with rings around it. The three main rings are called A, B, and C. Scientists say the rings are made of ice, dust, and rocks. These rings move around Saturn at different speeds.
