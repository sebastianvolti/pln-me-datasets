 photograph of a first aid kit First Aid Kit: A first aid kit is a very important box. There are many items inside of this kit.  image of a bandaid One kind of item inside of the kit is a bandage. Have you ever cut yourself? You probably put a bandage on the cut. Bandages cover up cuts to stop them from bleeding.  image of tweezers Another kind of item is a tweezer. A tweezer is a small tool. People use tweezers to pick up small things. People use tweezers to get splinters out of their skin. Splinters are tiny pieces of wood. A third kind of item is a manual. A manual tells people how to use the items in the kit.
