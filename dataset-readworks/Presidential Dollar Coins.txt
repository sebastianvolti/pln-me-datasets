These coins help Americans celebrate the presidents of the United States. These coins can be used for buying things, but they are more often used for decorations. And even though each coin is worth $1, one coin can be expensive to get. Some presidential dollar coins are sold for close to $300 each! Some people like to collect as many as they can.
