 You can play mini golf with parents and friends! Are you looking for a fun summer activity? Try mini golf! Mini golf is a smaller version of golf. In mini golf, you use a club to hit the golf ball. A club is a long metal or plastic stick with a head at the end. The goal of mini golf is to try to hit golf balls into holes. Many mini golf courses have obstacles that get in the way of the holes, so watch out!  Here is an animal-themed mini golf course. There are many mini golf courses in Florida. One mini golf course is called Smugglers Cove. This mini golf course has waterfalls and pirate ships. It also has real live alligators!
