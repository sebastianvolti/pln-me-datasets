   There are many memorials in Washington, D.C. Memorials are built to remember and honor people. Visitors go to the United States capital to see these memorials. The Jefferson Memorial honors President Thomas Jefferson. Inside this building is a nineteen-foot-high statue of the president. The Lincoln Memorial honors President Abraham Lincoln. Inside it is a statue of Lincoln sitting down. The Martin Luther King, Jr. Memorial is a large stone statue. The statue honors Martin Luther King, Jr. He worked for peace among all people. The Vietnam Veterans Memorial has two special walls. They are black walls that show the names of many soldiers. They fought and died for the United States in the Vietnam War.
