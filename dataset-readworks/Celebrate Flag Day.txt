   June 14 is Flag Day in America. Many people honor the American flag on that day. The American flag is red, white, and blue. It has 13 stripes. It has 50 stars. Each star stands for one of our 50 states. Where do we see the American flag? We see it in parades. We see it on people’s houses. We see it on buildings in towns. We see it at schools. We see the flag on many holidays. Where have you seen the American flag?
