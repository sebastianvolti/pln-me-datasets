   Lots of people ride on trains. Trains help people go from one place to another quickly. In some places, people ride on subway trains every day. A subway train is a train that goes underground. They can be found in cities around the world. Cities can have whole subway systems. That means they have lots of trains that go to different spots in the city. Cities have different names for their subway systems. The one in London is sometimes called the Tube. The one in Paris is called the Métro. The one in Boston is the T. And the one in New York City is just called the subway!
