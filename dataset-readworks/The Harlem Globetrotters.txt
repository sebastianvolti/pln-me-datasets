 This is a photo of the Harlem Globetrotters playing a game against another team. Have you heard of the Harlem Globetrotters? They are a famous basketball team. They are known for their amazing tricks and skills. Many of the NBA’s first Black players were on this team.  This is a photo of the Harlem Globetrotters performing. The team was created in 1926. They played their first game on January 7, 1927. Later, the team began adding fun tricks to their games. People loved watching them play! Today, they have played and performed in over 100 countries around the world. The Harlem Globetrotters are also known for doing good deeds. The team visits many schools during the year. They play fun games with students. They share important messages, like “stop bullying,” too.
