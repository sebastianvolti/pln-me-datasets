   People use money to buy things. They can buy goods and services. Goods are things. You can touch them. Clothes and food are goods. Toys are goods. Books are goods. Services are things people do for you. Having your teeth cleaned is a service. Getting a haircut is a service. Getting your car cleaned at a car wash is a service. Sometimes a good can be part of a service. If you go to a diner, a person cooks for you. That is a service. You also get food. Food is a good. Can you name any other goods and services?
