Credit: Denali National Park and Preserve, CC BY 2.0  Beavers are builders. They build dams. A dam is like a wall made of wood and mud. Beavers build dams in rivers. Beavers are swimmers. Their flat tails help them steer as they swim. Beavers can stay underwater for fifteen minutes! Beavers are rodents. Rodents are mammals with strong teeth. Mice and rats are also rodents. Beavers use their strong teeth to gnaw on trees. It’s easy to see that beavers are busy animals!
