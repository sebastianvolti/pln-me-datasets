     Martin Luther King Jr. was a leader. When he was young, there were many unfair laws. He worked to make laws fair. King taught people to treat one another with kindness. He was a very good speaker. He spoke to large groups of people. He told them about his dream. His dream was that all people would be treated fairly. He wanted all kinds of people to live together in peace. Today we honor Martin Luther King Jr. with a holiday. We celebrate his holiday every January.
