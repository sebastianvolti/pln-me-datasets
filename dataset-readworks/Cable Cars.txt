   Have you ever heard of a cable car? Cable cars help people get from place to place. Strong metal ropes pull them from place to place. That rope is the cable. Manjula Raman  Some cities have cable cars. Usually, these cable cars get pulled through the streets. They help people get to different places in the city. San Francisco is a city in California. It is famous for having cable cars. Cable cars are also used on mountains. If you have ever gone skiing, you might have used one. These cable cars are high off the ground! They hang from the cables. They get pulled up and down mountains. They help people get to the tops of mountains.
