   People today could not live without the ocean. We need it for lots of things. Here are some examples of what we get from the ocean. People get food from the ocean. We catch fish to sell, buy, and eat. People travel on the ocean to get to other places. Also, we can send things long distances on big boats. This is important for businesses and trade. People get medicine from the ocean, too. Some animals that live in the ocean make helpful chemicals. We can use these chemicals to make medicine. Plus, people can breathe because of the ocean! People breathe a gas called oxygen. Tiny creatures in the ocean make most of the oxygen on the earth.
