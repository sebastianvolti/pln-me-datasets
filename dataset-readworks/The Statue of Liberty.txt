 Statue of Liberty The Statue of Liberty is a statue of a woman. A statue is a piece of art. Statues are made in the shape of humans or animals. The Statue of Liberty has a crown on her head. She is holding a torch high above her head. The Statue of Liberty is a symbol of freedom. The word “liberty” means freedom. France gave the United States of America the Statue of Liberty. It was as a gift. France is a country in Europe. France wanted to give the United States this gift to celebrate their friendship.
