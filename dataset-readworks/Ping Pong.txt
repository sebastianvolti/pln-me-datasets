 A ping pong player reaches for the ball.  ping pong paddles and a ping pong ball Have you heard of table tennis? You may also know it as ping pong! In ping pong, two or four players hit a light ball back and forth on a table. They use small paddles. It looks like a smaller version of tennis. Everyone can play this sport, including children and adults. Ping pong is a very popular sport in China. Many Chinese parks and schools have ping pong tables. Chinese players have the most Olympic medals for ping pong. Almost 10 million Chinese people play ping pong in competitions! Have you played ping pong?
