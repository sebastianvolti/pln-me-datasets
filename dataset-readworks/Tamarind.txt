   A tamarind is a fruit. Have you ever tasted one? You might have tasted one without knowing it. The tamarind is popular in India and Mexico. It is used in lots of foods there. So if you’ve had Indian or Mexican food, you might have tasted one. It tastes sweet and sour at the same time. Before the fruit can go into a meal, it has to grow. It grows in groups on trees. A tamarind looks a little like a fat brown bean pod. It has a few seeds inside it. There is pulp around the seeds. The pulp is the juicy part of the fruit. That’s the part that tastes sweet and sour.
