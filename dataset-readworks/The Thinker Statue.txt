 The Thinker Statue The photo here shows a famous statue. The statue is a naked man sitting on a rock. The man is very fit. He looks like an athlete. You can see he is resting his chin on his hand. It seems like he is carefully thinking about something. His eyes seem to be looking down. This statue is called The Thinker. Why might it be called this? There are different statues like this one. They come in different sizes. The first statue of The Thinker was made by a French artist. The statue is meant to celebrate people’s ability to think and make creative things. This statue is known around the world.
