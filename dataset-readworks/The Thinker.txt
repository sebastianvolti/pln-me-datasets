Rodin Museum, Philadelphia: Bequest of Jules E. Mastbaum, F1929-7-15Conceived 1880; cast by 1926BronzeHeight: 27 inches (68.9 cm)AUGUSTE RODINFrench, 1840–1917   This is a famous sculpture. It is called The Thinker. It was made by a French artist. His name was Auguste Rodin. The Thinker shows a man sitting alone on a rock. He is deep in thought. He leans forward. His elbow is on his knee. His hand is holding up his chin. And his eyes are looking downward. This bronze sculpture stands for the creative mind at work.
